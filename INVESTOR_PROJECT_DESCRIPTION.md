## üéØ PRODUCT DESCRIPTION

### What LegalNexus Does

LegalNexus is a cloud-based SaaS platform that:

1. **Accepts Legal Queries**: Lawyers enter natural language questions or case descriptions
2. **Searches Intelligently**: AI analyzes meaning, relationships, and context (not just keywords)
3. **Ranks Results**: Returns top-5 most relevant precedents with 92% accuracy
4. **Provides Analysis**: Automatically generates comparative legal analysis
5. **Visualizes Connections**: Shows how cases connect through judges, courts, and statutes
